# Business Rules & Domain Logic

## 🏢 Multi-Tenancy
- The system is strictly multi-tenant. 
- A `Tenant` represents the SaaS customer (e.g., a Hotel Chain).
- A `Property` represents a physical hotel/resort owned by the Tenant.
- Users, Guests, Rooms, and Reservations are strictly bound to a `Tenant` (and usually a specific `Property`).
- Cross-tenant data access is strictly forbidden.

## 👥 Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
- Users are assigned a `Role` (e.g., General Manager, Front Desk, Housekeeping).
- Roles are assigned `Permissions` (e.g., `reservation.create`, `payment.process`).
- Middleware must verify both Role and Permission before allowing sensitive actions.

## 🛏️ Room & Reservation Logic
- A `Room` has a status (AVAILABLE, OCCUPIED, MAINTENANCE, CLEANING).
- A `Reservation` can include MULTIPLE rooms (handled via `ReservationRoom` join table).
- Check-in changes Room status to OCCUPIED. Check-out changes it to CLEANING/AVAILABLE.
- Availability checking must account for existing reservations overlapping the requested dates.

## 💳 Payments & Audits
- Payments are tied to Reservations. A reservation can have multiple partial payments.
- Total paid must be tracked against the reservation total to determine if it's fully paid.
- Every critical action (create, update, delete, check-in, payment) must generate an `AuditLog` entry.
